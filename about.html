About Boomerang Strategy Games

Michael Sawyer is a game designer, commercial drone pilot and Marine Scientist living in Perth, Western Australia, Australia.

Michael Sawyer is a game designer, commercial drone pilot and Marine Scientist living in Perth, Western Australia, Australia.

Michael grew up playing Monopoly, Risk, Hero Quest as well as a variety of PC and console games. His favourite genre is strategy and in 2015 he started designing his own board game, Vengence of the Gods. Originally it started as a bit of fun but after a few years of testing and refinement, the game became popular among his gaming group who encouraged him to start a business and fund it on Kickstarter.

Fast Forward a few years, several hundreds people have played it over 500 times and the game is nearing completion. In January 2019 Michael studied a business and marketing course and in March 2019, Boomerang Strategy Games was founded.
